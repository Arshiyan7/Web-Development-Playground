David Goggins: Master Your Mind, Defy the Odds

David Goggins is a retired U.S. Navy SEAL, an ultra-endurance athlete, and a highly sought-after motivational speaker and author. His life is a testament to the power of mental toughness and self-mastery, chronicled in his best-selling memoir, Can't Hurt Me. He is the only person to complete SEAL training (including three "Hell Weeks"), Army Ranger School, and Air Force Tactical Air Controller training. A former Guinness World Record holder for pull-ups, Goggins has completed over 60 ultra-marathons, triathlons, and ultra-triathlons. His philosophy revolves around the "40% Rule," which asserts that when you think you're done, you're actually only at 40% of your true capability, urging people to embrace suffering and push far past their perceived limits.
